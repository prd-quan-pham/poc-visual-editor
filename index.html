Find Your Fit

Nursing

Explore Essentia Health’s wide range of fulfilling nursing opportunities in Minnesota, North Dakota or Wisconsin. Whether you’re an aspiring or experienced nurse, you’ll find a position that suits your interests and skills.

Physician & Clinician

When you practice medicine at Essentia Health, you contribute to a collaborative, physician-led system that values the perspective of patients and their families. Enjoy opportunities to provide quality care to people of all ages, backgrounds, and needs across Minnesota, North Dakota, and Wisconsin.

Allied Health

Essentia Health offers exciting allied health careers within multiple clinical settings. As part of the patients care team, allied health roles provide diagnostic, therapeutic, and treatment for patients.  And with great people, excellent benefits, and the chance to live and work in a variety of fine communities, this could be the chance to get the most out of life, and your career! 

Operations & Administration Careers

It takes a strong operations team to keep a health care system functioning. Professionals provide data analysis, policy development, strategy, and leadership to ensure Essentia Health is providing quality care while executing on our values to give coordinated, high-quality care at a low cost.

Support Services

Non-medical roles that support what is needed to make our hospitals, clinics, and other facilities function. Explore entry level and career positions that help with maintenance, facilities, nutrition services, security, guest services, and environmental services. Personnel in these roles live out our value of hospitality every day.

You can be authentically you at Essentia Health. We’re proud to employ an incredible group of team members, and respect differences in age, gender, sexual orientation, disability status, background and religious beliefs.

Life At Essentia

Furthermore, we strive to be more than “just a job.” We’re actively engaged in improving our communities – whether that means mentoring children, adopting struggling schools, supporting animal shelters, or participating in local charity events.

If this sounds like the kind of family you want to be a part of, come join us. We’re always looking for dynamic, motivated individuals who are yearning to elevate the health of the communities we serve.
